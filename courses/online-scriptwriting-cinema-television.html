Online Scriptwriting for Cinema and Television

5/5 Reviews

The art of scriptwriting forms the foundation of every successful cinematic and television project. At DBIMA Paris, we are thrilled to introduce our 6-Month Online Scriptwriting Course, a comprehensive program designed for aspiring writers who wish to transform their ideas into compelling, industry-standard scripts. This course is tailored for individuals with a passion for storytelling, enabling them to bridge the gap between creative concepts and professional script development.

Vision

Our vision is to inspire and support individuals passionate about storytelling in cinema and television, helping them achieve their dreams by introducing them to the art and craft of scriptwriting and equipping them to excel in the entertainment industry.

learning objectives

  • Develop a deep understanding of scriptwriting principles, from idea conception to final script delivery.
  • Master the craft of storytelling and narrative structure tailored for cinema and television.
  • Gain proficiency in writing scripts in industry-standard formats.
  • Learn to pitch, present, and refine scripts for professional opportunities.
  • Understand the nuances of working in multicultural film industries, with a focus on both Western and Indian styles.
  • Complete a polished short film script ready for production.

The Cost of Filmmaking in Paris

Producing a film in Paris can be financially daunting due to the high costs of locations, permits, equipment, and crew. However, the Filmmaking in Paris program is designed to offer an affordable way for students to create their films in the city. In collaboration with our partnering institutions, DBIMA offers financial and logistical support to help students manage production costs, making the experience more accessible while maintaining a high standard of production.
